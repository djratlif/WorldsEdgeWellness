My Therapeutic Approach

I believe therapy works best when it's collaborative, relational, and grounded in safety. I strive to create a space where you feel supported, respected, and free to show up as you are. Rather than using a one-size-fits-all model, I tailor therapy to your needs, drawing from evidence-based practices while staying attuned to the whole person.

My approach is trauma-informed and integrative, with an emphasis on emotional regulation, self-understanding, and strengthening relationships. I value warmth, curiosity, and authenticity in the therapeutic process, and I believe meaningful growth happens when you feel both supported and empowered.
