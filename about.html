About Megan McCraw

Licensed Clinical Social Worker dedicated to creating a safe, supportive space for healing and personal growth in Hendersonville, North Carolina.

Hello, I'm Megan

Welcome to Worlds Edge Wellness. I'm so glad you're here, and I want you to know that taking this step to learn more about therapy shows incredible courage and self-awareness.

For over 8 years, I've had the privilege of walking alongside individuals, couples, and families as they navigate life's challenges and work toward healing and growth. My approach to therapy is rooted in the belief that every person has inherent worth and the capacity for positive change.

I understand that reaching out for help can feel vulnerable and overwhelming. That's why I've dedicated my practice to creating a warm, non-judgmental space where you can feel safe to explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences at your own pace.

My Therapeutic Philosophy

My approach to therapy is grounded in respect, compassion, and the belief that healing happens within the context of a trusting therapeutic relationship.

Core Beliefs

I believe that every person is the expert on their own life and has the inherent capacity for growth and healing. My role is to provide a safe, supportive environment where you can explore your experiences, gain new insights, and develop the tools you need to create positive change.

Therapy is a collaborative process. While I bring professional training and experience, you bring your unique perspective, strengths, and wisdom about your own life. Together, we'll work toward your goals at a pace that feels comfortable for you.

My Approach

I use an integrative approach, drawing from various evidence-based therapeutic modalities to best meet your individual needs. This might include cognitive-behavioral techniques, mindfulness practices, trauma-informed interventions, or family systems approaches.

I'm particularly passionate about helping clients develop emotional regulation skills, improve their relationships, and build resilience. I believe in addressing not just symptoms, but the whole person ‚Äì including your strengths, values, and life goals.
